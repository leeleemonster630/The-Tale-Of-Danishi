Zygaria, The Forgotten Divine.

Zygaria, known as The Forgotten Divine, is a city of great significance rooted in ancient myth. Millions of years ago, it was barren land until demigods settled there, imbuing it with life and divine power. As humans migrated to Zygaria seeking refuge, they worshipped the demigods, and Alphos, the eldest and most powerful god, moved by their devotion, built radiant cities of stone and light. From his power, he created the Crescent Prism—a sacred relic tied to the moon that granted no more than two wishes each lunar cycle. Peace endured until a man began to question the gods, accusing them of withholding their power. His defiance spread among the people, and Alphos, angered, banished him and refused to use the Prism again. In rebellion, the man struck the relic, shattering it. The fractured Prism still held divine power, and its pieces were taken and forged into weapons, turning humanity against the gods and igniting the Zygarian War. The war devastated both sides, ending with Alphos's defeat by those same divine weapons. In the aftermath, the remaining gods abandoned Zygaria—some fleeing beyond the realm, others hiding among humans. The city was eventually rebuilt, and its history became legend taught within the citadel. Some dismiss it as a myth, while others believe the demigods still walk among them.
