Dani, real name Danishi, lives in the city of Zygaria, a place once home to powerful demigods. No one really knows what happened to them, only that they all vanished after the Great War. Dani is now 18, and she feels herself changing---becoming something different and powerful. After a great tragedy, something in her erupted...and she learns that she is half demigod and has the power of The Abyssal Aria, a sound that is so strong it can destroy anything in its path. As Dani struggles to control her ability, she also begins to uncover the truth about Zygaria’s past—and the dangers that still exist within it. With tensions rising and hidden forces emerging from the shadows, she uses her power not only as a weapon but as a means to protect others, confronting those who threaten to plunge the city back into chaos.
